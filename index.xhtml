Mathematics Graduate Student Association

Representing and serving mathematics graduate students at the University of Notre Dame

Purpose

The Mathematics Graduate Student Association (MGSA) is the democratic organization of Notre Dame's math grad students. It works to ensure good studying and working conditions for students, serves as a point of contact for the department and broader Graduate Student Union, runs the graduate student seminar, and organizes occasional social events. You can look at our constitution, if you're into that sort of thing.

Graduate Student Seminar

During the academic year, the MGSA holds a regular seminar of talks given by graduate students. Find information about this seminar here.
