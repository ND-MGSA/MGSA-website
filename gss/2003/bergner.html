Graduate Student Seminar, 4:45pm October 27, 2003

Speaker:

Julie Bergner

Title:

A Taste of Homotopy

Abstract:

In this talk I will begin by introducing the basic concepts of homotopy theory of topological spaces. From there I will take brief excursions into ideas that have developed from them, such as stable homotopy theory, model category structures, and simplicial objects.
