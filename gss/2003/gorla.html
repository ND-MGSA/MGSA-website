Graduate Student Seminar, 5:00pm April 30, 2004; HH127

Speaker:

Elisa Gorla

Title:

Curves, general plane sections and cohomology

Abstract:

I will discuss what one can say about a curve in projective space by looking at its general plane section, i.e. its intersection with a general plane. I will try to motivate why looking at a general section makes sense in our contest, and how it relates to vanishing of the first cohomology of the ideal sheaf of the curve.
