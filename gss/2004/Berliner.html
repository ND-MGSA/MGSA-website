Graduate Student Seminar, 5:00 pm January 17, 2005; HH229

Speaker:

Allegra Berliner

Title:

For the love of manifolds

Abstract:

I will present a top-ten list of reasons that people love manifolds, from their natural role in calculus, to classification of surfaces, to cool invariants from geometry, topology, and surgery theory.
