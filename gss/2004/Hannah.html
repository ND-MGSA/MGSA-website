Graduate Student Seminar, 4:30 pm November 22, 2004; HH127

Speaker:

Heather Hannah

Title:

On the Cauchy Problem for the KdV Equation

Abstract:

Using appropriate analytic initial data, we shall construct nonanalytic complex-valued solutions to the initial value problem for the KdV equation.
