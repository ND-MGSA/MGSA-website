Graduate Student Seminar, 5:00 pm April 7, 2003, Hayes-Healy 231

Speaker:

Title:

A Brief Introduction to the Index Theorem

Abstract:

I will introduce the notion of a genus from a topological view. We will then see how the A^ genus can be viewed as the index of the Dirac operator via the Atiyah-Singer index theorem and how this can be applied to other issues, such as the existence of positive scalar curvature metrics.
