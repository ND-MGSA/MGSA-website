Graduate Student Seminar, 5:00 pm Tuesday, October 15, 2002, Hayes-Healy 229

Speaker:

Dan Jackson

Title:

Rationality of the zeta-function of a hypersurface defined over a finite field

Abstract:

We will discuss the zeta-function of a hypersurface over a finite field and explore the role of p-adic analysis in the proof of the rationality of this function. With the exception of some facts from basic algebra, all of the required background material will be introduced.
