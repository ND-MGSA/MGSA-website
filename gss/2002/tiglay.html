Graduate Student Seminar, 5:00pm April 14, 2003, Hayes-Healy 231

Speaker:

Feride Tiglay

Title:

The motion of an incompressible fluid in two dimensions

Abstract:

The Cauchy problem for the Euler equations of hydrodynamics is well posed locally in time. In two dimensions, the solution exists for all time. I will try to sketch the basic ideas in a proof of the existence of solutions for all time in two dimensions
