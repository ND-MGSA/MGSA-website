Graduate Student Seminar, 4:30pm March 24, 2003, Hayes-Healy 229

Vesta Coufal

Title:

An Introduction to Fixed Point Theory

Abstract:

I will present a brief description of some aspects of fixed point theory, from the point of view of topology. Some detail will be given regarding the issues on which I am currantly working.
