Graduate Student Seminar, 4:30pm February 3, 2003, Hayes-Healy 229

Speaker:

Cabral Balreira

Title:

Spectral Analysis of Schrodinger Operators

Abstract:

In Quantum Physics, the movement of particles under a potential is described by Schrodinger Operators. In this talk, we would like to address the problem of non-recurrent motion in Quantum Physics. We will start with the basic notions of Spectral Analysis and a discussion of the Laplacian Operator. Our goals are to State Lavine's Theorem on repulsive interactions and discuss its possible extensions.
