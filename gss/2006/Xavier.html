Graduate Student Seminar, 4:00 pm August 28, 2006; HH229

Speaker:

Prof. Fred Xavier

 

Title:

A non-linear view of linear algebra

 

Abstract:

We will see how ideas from geometry and dynamics can be used to recast certain aspects of linear algebra as special cases of more general results in non-linear analysis.
