Graduate Student Seminar, 4:30 pm February 6, 2006; HH229

Speaker:

Sasha Lyapina

 

Title:

Elements of Poisson Geometry

 

Abstract:

The talk will be devoted to a discussion of the definition of a Poisson structure on a smooth manifold. Some examples and applications will be considered. In particular, we will see how this structure naturally appears and works in classical mechanics.
