Graduate Student Seminar, 4:30 pm March 27, 2006; HH229

Speaker:

Ray Jensen

 

Title:

Covariance and invariance under conformal transformations of a Riemannian manifold with measure

 

Abstract:

We will begin with a review of conformal transformations of a metric g associated to a manifold M. Then we will look at conformally covariant operators and geometric quantities invariant under conformal transformation. Finally, we will introduce a new measure m on M and define some new invariants.
