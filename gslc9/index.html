9th Annual Graduate Student Logic Conference

April 26-27, 2008
University of Notre Dame

This conference is hosted by graduate students for graduate students in all areas of logic.

There will be scheduled talks all day Saturday and Sunday morning. Everyone is invited to attend a group dinner Saturday night. There will be an unofficial get-together Friday evening.

Most people will probably arrive on Friday and leave Sunday afternoon. (Travel to Notre Dame)
