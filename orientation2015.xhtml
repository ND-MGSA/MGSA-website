2015 Math Department Orientation

Welcome!

Welcome to Notre Dame! The Department of Mathematics has organized an orientation from Friday, August 21 to Monday, August 24. On this page you will find a tentative schedule, as well as some additional material for the orientation.

Most of the orientation's daytime events will take place in Hurley Hall (coordinates) (Google maps).

All mathematics graduate students are welcome to attend the talks and social events. For questions or comments please contact Brian or Alexander.

You may also be interested in the websites for the Graduate School Orientation and the International Student Orientation.
